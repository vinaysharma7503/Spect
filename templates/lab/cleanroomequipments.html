Cleanroom Equipments


Spectevo offers cleanroom equipments within the Cleanroom for Biotech Pharmaceuticals, Microelectronics, Laboratory, Semi conductor units and Hospitals. We provide tailor made solution and design cleanroom equipments for specific use. The equipments are designed with durability, ease of use, compactness and aesthetics in focus. Our design engineers have incorporated many small features that differentiates our products. Our product standardization ensures the deliveries are faster, and also enables our clients, easy access to the spares of each component. Our design engineers have also worked to ensure these equipments integrate easily with our cleanroom panels.
