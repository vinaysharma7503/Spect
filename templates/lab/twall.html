Triple Wall Glass Reactor With Ring Baffle & Without Ring Baffle


The Glass reactors come in various sizes and various vessel options. The sizes range from 300mL to 5L. The triple wall reactor also comes in variety of vessel options. Triple Wall with Ring Baffle, Triple Wall, Double Wall with Ring Baffle, Double Wall. From 10L to 60L (Triple Wall with Ring Baffle) , 100L (Double Wall with Ring Baffle), 300L (Double Wall, Custom-made).

The reactors are provided with Leak-proof flush valve. The pressure sensitive spring loaded flush valve prevents leakage. There is no dead space.

The 2 main types of the reactors are:
• Bench-top reactor
• Mini and Pilot Plant reactors

Ring Baffle Technology Uniquely Engineered For Precise Temperature Control:

Ring baffle technology forces the thermal fluid in a specific path to ensure heat distribution while maximizing the working surface area promoting even heat transfer by providing rapid temperature recovery due to endothermic or exothermic reactions quickly and efficiently while maximizing the energy provided by temperature control unit.

Less Time And More Productivity

Precise and uniform temperature control will improve productivity by decreasing process time while increasing higher yields and reducing operating cost of the process.

Precise Temperature Process Control With Integrated Glass Ring Baffle Technology. Ring Baffle Technology Benefits:
• Maximizes the heat transfer area
• Integrated full jacket design
• Optimal heat transfer
• Eliminates hot/cold zones
• Maximizes the energy of the TCU

Even Heating/Cooling

Ring Baffle prevents “Short-path” of HTF. Optimizes the Heat Transfer to the Internal Vessel Uniform and Efficient temperature stability.
