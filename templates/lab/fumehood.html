Fume Hoods


A fume hood is a type of local ventilation device that is designed to limit exposure to hazardous or noxious fumes, vapors or dust.

We offer a complete line of benchtop, walk-in, and teaching hoods, including a wide selection of hood accessories, cabinets, countertops and blowers. Our Fume Hoods combine effective control, from exposure to hazardous chemicals and agents used in the laboratory.

With a reputation for exceptional value, our fume hoods are found in industrial, pharmaceutical and laboratories across the country.
