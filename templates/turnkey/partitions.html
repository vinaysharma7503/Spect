MODULAR PARTITIONS


Cleanrooms are unique in their construction, having virtually every component built in during production to suit all applications and to meet industrial requirements.

Spectevo offers a complete range of cleanroom partition and ceiling systems. These systems meet the technical requirements of high specification controlled environments such as Pharmaceutical, Biotech and Microelectronics, as well as Food-processing plants. The ceiling panels are non-particle shedding, load bearing, walkable panels and feature pre-formed cut outs for the housing of HEPA filters and other dedicated services.
