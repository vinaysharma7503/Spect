Walls


A vital ingredient in the creation of Cleanrooms, Wall panels form a substantial part of our product offerings. Spectevo wall panels are manufactured in standard sizes and can also be customized depending on the requirement.

Specifications:
• Standard size: 1200 X 3000mm (Other sizes customizable on request)
• MOC: GI Pre-Coated / GI powder-coated / SS -304
• Thickness: 0.6 / 0.8 / 1mm
• Skin Type: GPSP sheet / Aluminium sheet
• Panel Thickness: 50 / 80 / 100mm
• Fastened to extruded aluminium / GI profiles
• Fire retardant PUF as In-Fill (Other In-Fill options available on request)
• Factory- made cutouts for integration with Pass boxes, Autoclaves etc
• Factory- made cutouts for in-built power sockets and Magnehelic manometers
